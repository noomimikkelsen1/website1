Our Story

Pioneering the science of melanoidins.

Founded in Denmark in 2016, Kaffe Bueno began by asking a simple question: what if the world's most overlooked by-products held its most valuable molecules?

From upcycling to technology platform.

Kaffe Bueno was founded in Denmark in 2016 by a team of Colombian entrepreneurs, including Camilo Fernandez and Alejandro Franco, who grew up seeing coffee as far more than a caffeinated beverage. They set out to unleash the untapped potential of coffee by-products and work toward a more circular, sustainable society.

Along the way, we found a molecular family with extraordinary potential: melanoidins — complex, bioactive biopolymers. Today, that science defines us.

A science company, not an ingredient supplier.

Our heritage is in upcycling. Our future is in melanoidin technology — a scalable platform that can expand into new molecules, applications, and industries.

We combine deep science, sustainability, and industrial scalability to lead an emerging category with significant commercial and scientific potential.
