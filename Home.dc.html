The molecule

An emerging category of bioactive molecules.

Melanoidins are complex, high-molecular-weight biopolymers formed through the Maillard reaction. For decades they were treated as a by-product. We see a platform.

Using green chemistry, Kaffe Bueno isolates and characterises melanoidins as multifunctional actives — antioxidant, anti-glycation, skin-hydrating, and SPF-boosting — with clinically proven performance in beauty and personal care.
